CITYVOICE

A place based call-in system for gathering, sharing, and understanding community feedback.

Accessible

Anyone with access to a telephone can participate with CityVoice. Access to, or knowledge of, computers or smart phones is unnecessary. Just call in and speak.

Engaging

The human voice is a rich medium for communication. Combining it with digital technology is a powerful way to connect with people.

Robust

Collect opinions in a structured manner and quickly understand the underlying thinking and sentiment behind those opinions. Follow up with citizens easily.

South Bend's
Vacant and Abandoned
Property Initiative

CityVoice is being used to engage residents in South Bend around vacant and abandoned properties. The feedback collected is helping the City prioritize its actions at the property level for over 1,000 properties.
